About Us

Tinapay ni Bai started as a small neighborhood bakery in the heart of General Santos City, driven by a passion for crafting the perfect bread. What began as a family recipe passed down through generations has grown into a beloved local destination for freshly baked goods.

Every morning, our bakers arrive before dawn to prepare each loaf, roll, and pastry by hand. We use only the finest locally sourced ingredients — real butter, farm-fresh eggs, and premium flour — because we believe great bread starts with great ingredients.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

What are your operating hours?

We are open Monday through Saturday from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and major holidays so our bakers can rest and recharge.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We deliver across General Santos City. Orders placed before 10:00 AM are delivered the same day. A flat delivery fee of ₱49.00 applies to all orders.

Can I place bulk orders for events?

Absolutely. We cater to birthdays, corporate events, and weddings. Please contact us at least 3 days in advance for bulk orders so we can prepare everything fresh for your event.

Are your products suitable for people with allergies?

Most of our products contain wheat, dairy, and eggs. We currently do not offer a dedicated gluten-free line, but we're working on it. Please let us know about specific allergies when you order.

How do I create an account?

Click the "Log In" button in the top navigation bar, then switch to the "Sign Up" tab. Enter a username and password to create your account. Once registered, you can log in and start placing orders immediately.
